Our drafting service is focussed on the drafting of technical and structural building plans prepared for municipal council submission. Service includes:

  • The drafting of all plans
  • Signing off of plans by a SACAP registered Architect
  • Submission of Building plans

The Drafting of all building plans

We Draft Building plans for municipal submission. Every care is taken to prepare your plans to the best standard possible to ensure that all submissions go as smoothly and cost effectively as possible.

Signing off of plans by a SACAP registered Architect

We provide a service where plans are checked and signed off by a SACAP registered architect to ensure that all plans are correct and valid

Submission of Building plans

We provide a service where we will submit your plans to your local municipality and ensure that the process is as painless and quick as possible.
